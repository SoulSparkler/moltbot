# Trésor Tendance TikTok Posting Skill

This skill converts Etsy listings into TikTok-ready content for the Trésor Tendance vintage shop.

## Inputs

Typical inputs:

- listing_title
- listing_description
- image_url
- product_type
- historical_notes (optional)

## Outputs

Always generate:

### Hook

A strong opening line for the TikTok video.

### Caption

A short caption suitable for TikTok.

### Hashtags

3–8 relevant hashtags.

### Video Idea

Simple idea for filming the item.

### Voiceover (optional)

Short narration under 60 words.

## Tone

Tone should be:

- discovery-driven
- curious
- storytelling oriented

Avoid:

- corporate language
- long paragraphs
- exaggerated claims

Focus on:

- vintage charm
- visual details
- why the object is interesting

## Example Structure

Hook:
"You won't believe what I found in a French brocante."

Caption:
"A small piece of French history hiding in plain sight."

Hashtags:
#vintagefind #frenchvintage #brocante #vintagetiktok #etsyshop

Video Idea:
Start with a close-up detail shot, then reveal the full object in natural light.
